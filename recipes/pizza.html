Pizza Recipe

This is a homemade pizza, which is easy to make. This pizza may not be very healthy but it is surely better than the pizzas which are usually bought. This pizza can be made with your family, friends or on your own. It is a great activity to take your mind off of something, and it is a great family bonding activity, There is many different variants to make this pizza depending on what toppings you would like to use, but in this recipe pepperoni is used.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Cornmeal
  • Sugar (To tenderize the dough)

Steps

  1. Mix the warm water, yeast and granulated sugar in a bowl, and let it sit in a bowl. Allow it to rest in a bowl for 5 mins, and after mix the dough with a wooden spoon
  2. Add the olive oil, salt and flour, and let it beat for 2 minutes
  3. Keep mixing the dough for 5 additional minutes, if the dough becomes too sticky, you can sprinkle some flour, after kneading the dough should become soft, poke it with a toothpick and if it rises slowly, it should be ready.
  4. Lightly grease a large bowl with oil, and place the dough in the bowl.Cover it with something and let it rise for about 60-90 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven to about 250Â°C, allow it to heat for 15 minutes while you shape the pizza, lightly grease the pizza pan and light sprinkle cornmeal. (This gives the pizza extra crust, crunch and flavour)
  6. Shape the dough and make sure to push down on it, to release any air bubbles. After add the toppings.
  7. Place the pizza in the oven and bake it for around 13-15 minutes. (Tip:Brush the top with olive oil)
  8. When it is done, Slice the pizza and enjoy!
