Donut Recipe

Glazed Donuts, these are an amazing and tasty treat to take when craving something sweet. They are easy and cheap to make, although they are Glazed in this recipe, they could be made with chocolate also, but in this recipe we are mainly focusing on Glazed Donuts.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt and Vanilla Extract
  • Nutmeg
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Preparing the Dough - Whisk the warm milk, yeast and a tablespoon of sugar togheter. Cover and allow it to sit for 5-10 minute, until foamy
  2. Add the remaining sugar, eggs, butter, vanilla, nutmeg, salt and about 250g of flour, beat on low speed for a minute. After add the remaining flour and beat on medium speed until the dough comes togheter
  3. Knead the Dough -Keep the dough in the mixer and beat for an additional 5-7 full minutes. After kneading, the dough should be soft, you can poke it with your finger, and if it slowly comes back up, it should be ready to be put to rise.
  4. Let Dough Rise -Lightly grease a large bowl with oil, place the dough inside it, and cover. Let the dough rise for about 1.5-2 hours. Preferably until it doubles in size
  5. Shape Doughnuts -When the dough is ready, punch it down to remove any air bubbles. After remove the dough from the bowl and turn it out onto a lightly floured surface. Using a rolling pin, roll the dough out until it is about 1.5cm thick. Now using a doughnut cutter, cut it into doughnuts.
  6. After line 1 or 2 baking sheets with parchment paper, and place the dougnuts. Make sure to cover them and leave them there for about 30 minutes as you heat the oil.(They should rise abit as they are resting)
  7. Preparing Oil -Pour Oil into a large, heavy duty pot fitted with an oil thermometer. Turn the stove on to medium heat, and heat the oil until 200Â°C
  8. Cooking the Doughnuts -Add about 2-3 doughnuts at a time and cook for one minute on each side, repeat this with all the doughnuts
  9. Preparing the Glaze-Whisk all the glaze Ingredients togheter, and dip eachWARMdoughnut into the glaze, make sure to glaze all the sides, after around 20 minutes the glaze should set.
  10. You can now eat and enjoy!(Preferably doughnuts should be eaten on the same day)
